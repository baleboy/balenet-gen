My first 'Dollar' in the App Store

9 July 2026 Devlog

A few months ago I released my Helsinki public transport widget to the Apple App Store. It's a niche utility app that I did for myself and my friends, and I never fooled myself into thinking that I could make money out of it. But as an experiment, I added an in-app purchase to unlock unlimited number of favorite stops.

Lo and behold, although the app has less than 100 users someone actually paid for it! In another time I would have framed this first "Dollar" and put it up on the wall, but in this day an age the best I can do is post evidence of the financial transaction here:
